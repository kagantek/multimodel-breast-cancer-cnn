About This Project

This project is a part of a graduation thesis focusing on deploying multiple deep learning models trained on different medical imaging datasets. Each model is designed to detect abnormalities and provide predictive diagnostics for a specific type of medical image: Mammography, Ultrasound, and Histopathology.

Users can interact with each model by uploading relevant images and receiving AI-driven predictions to support medical decision-making and early diagnosis.
