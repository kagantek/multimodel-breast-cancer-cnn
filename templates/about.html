About This Project

This graduation project presents a comparative analysis of convolutional neural networks (CNNs) applied to medical imaging data. The primary aim is to evaluate the baseline performance of well-known deep learning architectures across three imaging modalities: Mammography, Ultrasound, and Histopathology.
